コネクションズ (Konections)

A Japanese version of the New York Times' viral word connection game

About This Project

This project is a JavaScript-based game based on the New York Times' viral word connection game "Connections," adapted to Japanese. I named it "Konections" with a K since in Japanese Roman lettering, "K" is often used instead of "C".

How To Play

The goal is to group 16 Japanese words into 4 categories of 4 related words each.

海 (sea)
山 (mountain)
砂漠 (desert)
森 (forest)

These would be grouped as natural landscapes
