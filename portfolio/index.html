About Me

Nodoka Shibasaki is an Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) major with an interest in combining hardware and software to solve engineering problems. She focuses on areas like robotics, healthcare, and aerospace, with a particular interest in circuit design, motor control, and system integration. Nodoka enjoys learning through hands-on projects and working with teams to build practical solutions.

She has worked on projects such as designing wearable robotics and developing tools for gait analysis. These experiences have helped her grow her technical skills while learning the value of collaboration and persistence.

In her free time, Nodoka leads runs with the university's women’s running club on Sunday mornings and plays intramural basketball, where she’s the only woman on her team. These activities reflect her enjoyment of staying active and connecting with others. She looks forward to continuing to develop her skills and contributing to engineering projects that help others.
