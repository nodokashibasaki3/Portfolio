Hip Exoskeleton Research

During my time working on hip exoskeleton research, I focused on improving the design and functionality of the device to enhance mobility for users. My contributions included calibrating sensors to ensure accurate data collection and assembling wiring to create a reliable system for operation.

Throughout this project, I was able to learn PCB design, circuits and signal design, soldering and the use of detachable connectors.

This experience allowed me to deepen my understanding of wearable robotics and gain hands-on experience in both hardware and software aspects of the project.
